About iPhone charge speeds

Find out how to improve the charging speed of your iPhone.

Your iPhone may charge faster with a different charger

For faster wired charging, use a USB-C Power Delivery charger, and a USB-C cable for iPhone 15 models and later1 or a USB-Cto Lightning cable for iPhone 14models and earlier

Charging your iPhone may take longer than expected if you use:

  • A wired charger that provides 7.5W or less
  • A wireless charger that provides less than 10W, such as a Qi1-certified charger
